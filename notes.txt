printf format safety:
- For fixed-width signed integers (for example `int32_t`), include `<inttypes.h>` and use `PRId32`/`PRIi32`.
- For `%X`/`%x`, pass an `unsigned int` argument. Cast small-width values in variadic calls, e.g. `(unsigned)raw`.
- Avoid relying on implicit promotions in `printf`; mismatched format/argument types are undefined behavior in C.
